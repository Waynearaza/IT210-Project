About

The Cordillera Administrative Region

Nestled within the highest and largest mountain range in the Philippines, the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) is a land of misty peaks, ancient rice terraces, and vibrant indigenous traditions.

Often referred to as the "Roof of the Philippines," this landlocked highland sanctuary offers a cool escape and a deep dive into the ancestral domain of the Igorot people. Whether you are chasing the sea of clouds at Mt. Pulag, exploring the mystical hanging coffins of Sagada, or marveling at the sheer ingenuity of the Banaue Rice Terraces, the Cordilleras promise an unforgettable journey into the heart of Philippine heritage.
