Begnas Festival

Mountain Province

Harvest and Harmony

Begnas is an Igorot thanksgiving tradition honoring a bountiful harvest and the strength of the community.

Begnas Festival is one of the featured highlights of the Cordillera region and continues to attract both first-time visitors and returning travelers because of its cultural depth and scenic appeal.

What to Expect

You can expect meaningful local experiences, rich storytelling, and opportunities to connect with people and traditions that shape Cordillera identity.

Best Time to Go

Planning ahead helps you enjoy better weather, smoother travel, and fuller appreciation of local schedules and activities.
